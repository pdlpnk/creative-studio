# Creative Intent

Make the approved CTA feel like one clear next step: complete registration. In the first second, communicate a simple service action without promising a result, reward or financial outcome.

# Audience

Adults only (18+), TR, Registration funnel, banner language: TR.

# Approved Text

- Headline: Bilgileri net şekilde keşfedin
- Supporting copy: Not provided in the Creative Plan; do not add supporting copy.
- CTA: Detayları Gör

# Primary Hook

Clear mobile interface: Show a direct digital action.

# Scene

Modern premium smartphone in three-quarter perspective, screen facing viewer, occupying 30–35% of the canvas, with three large readable interface cards and no tiny pseudo-text. Supporting element: Two or three large abstract interface cards with simple shapes, generous spacing and no pseudo-text or metrics. One large rounded notification panel with a neutral action cue, no unread badge, countdown or fabricated message.

# Composition

Use headline_left_visual_right: headline, hero visual, CTA. headline 32%, visual 38%, CTA 10%, whitespace 20%. Headline area: left upper-to-middle; visual area: right 38%; CTA area: left lower. Keep 7% on all sides and no more than 3 text blocks. Reading flow: headline → phone screen → CTA.

# Visual Style

Palette: deep blue base, white text surface, cyan accent. Background: very faint modular grid that supports a digital interface without adding pseudo-data. Lighting: soft studio highlight on the hero visual with a grounded shadow; preserve strong text contrast. Style: clean, contemporary minimal advertising art direction. Emotion: clear hierarchy, bold headline and one direct action

# Must Avoid

- No snow, winter styling or Christmas cues.
- No additional offer text, amounts, bonuses, cashback, deadline, testimonial, payout, statistic or result claim.
- No minors, teen styling, real celebrities, protected brands, cash piles, gambling-as-work imagery or financial-dashboard UI.
- No guarantee, income, refund, no-loss or risk-free wording; no hidden material conditions.
- No tiny text, busy collage, unreadable pseudo-text, extra icons, watermark or cropped CTA.

# Output

Square 1080x1080, high readability, mobile-first, return only the image.
