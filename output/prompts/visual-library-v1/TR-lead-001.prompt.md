# Creative Intent

Make the approved CTA feel like one clear next step: start a support conversation. In the first second, communicate a simple service action without promising a result, reward or financial outcome.

# Audience

Adults only (18+), TR, Lead funnel, banner language: TR.

# Approved Text

- Headline: Bilgileri net şekilde keşfedin
- Supporting copy: Not provided in the Creative Plan; do not add supporting copy.
- CTA: Bilgi Al

# Primary Hook

Adult support manager: Make a lead conversation feel available and human.

# Scene

Clearly adult man, approximately mid-20s or older, holding a phone in three-quarter view with a calm, non-celebratory expression. Supporting element: One oversized chat card with two short neutral bubbles and no fabricated testimonial, count or urgency cue.

# Composition

Use person_right_offer_left: adult person, headline, CTA. person 42%, headline 28%, CTA 10%, whitespace 20%. Headline area: left upper; visual area: right 42%; CTA area: left lower. Keep 7% on all sides and no more than 3 text blocks. Reading flow: headline → human/chat cue → CTA.

# Visual Style

Palette: deep blue base, white text surface, cyan accent. Background: smooth two-tone studio gradient with a quiet centre and no busy pattern. Lighting: soft studio highlight on the hero visual with a grounded shadow; preserve strong text contrast. Style: clean, contemporary clean mobile-first advertising art direction. Emotion: calm, transparent and human; use open posture, white space and stable contrast

# Must Avoid

- No additional offer text, amounts, bonuses, cashback, deadline, testimonial, payout, statistic or result claim.
- No minors, teen styling, real celebrities, protected brands, cash piles, gambling-as-work imagery or financial-dashboard UI.
- No guarantee, income, refund, no-loss or risk-free wording; no hidden material conditions.
- No tiny text, busy collage, unreadable pseudo-text, extra icons, watermark or cropped CTA.

# Output

Square 1080x1080, high readability, mobile-first, return only the image.
